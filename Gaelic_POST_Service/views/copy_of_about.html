What is PoS Tagger

 

PoS Tagger is a Brill-based Scottish Gaelic Tokeniser and Part-of-Speech Tagger developed at the University of Edinburgh (Celtic and Scottish Studies).

 

This project is led by Alistair Lawson, with software development by Simon Garrelou in conjunction with Sammy Danso and William Lamb, at the University of Edinburgh.
